ThinkCentre M910q SSD Replacement

Follow these 10 simple steps to upgrade the SSD in your Lenovo ThinkCentre M910q. This guide will cover both dissasembly and reassembly.

Step 1

Remove the single screw from the rear of the system.

Step 2

Slide the cover toward the front, and lift it from the system.

Step 3

Flip the system over, slide the bottom cover towards the rear of the system, and remove it.

Step 4

Use a pair of tweezers to lift out the plastic pin from the SSD.

Step 5

Use your finger to lift the SSD out from underneath, and remove it from its slot.

Step 6

Insert the new SSD into the slot.

Step 7

Press the drive down, and insert the plastic pin to secure it.

Step 8

Install the bottom cover, and slide it towards the front of the unit.

Step 9

Flip the system over, install the top cover, and slide it towards the rear of the system.

Step 10

Insert the screw at the rear of the system, and screw it in.
